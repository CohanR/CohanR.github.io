Instructions

This viewer is using NiiVue to support on-the-fly 3D rendering. The "4th Pane" shows the 3D volume.

Controls:

  1. Left Click + Drag: Rotate 3D / Adjust brightness (2D)
  2. Right Click + Drag: Pan / Zoom
  3. Scroll Wheel: Change slice

To load your own image:

Simply Drag and Drop your .nii.gz file anywhere on the black viewer area.
