DICOM → NIfTI converter (runs in your browser)

Nothing uploads to a server. Conversion happens locally using @niivue/dicom-loader (dcm2niix WASM) + Niivue viewer. Drop a DICOM folder onto the viewer.

1) Load DICOMs
Drag-and-drop a DICOM folder onto the black viewer on the right.
If you drop outside the viewer, your browser may try to open files. This page blocks that, but still: aim for the viewer.
2) Notes
• If your “T1 DICOM folder” contains multiple series, dcm2niix will pick a series it can form into a volume. If nothing loads, your folder might be: (a) not a single series, (b) missing slices, or (c) contains only DICOMDIR + compressed assets your browser can’t read.
• Tested behaviour varies by browser. Chrome/Edge are usually best for folder drops.
