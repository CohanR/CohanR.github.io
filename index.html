I study how the brain changes and reorganises itself in health and disease using different non-invasive brain stimulation modalities such as transcranial magnetic stimulation and various neuroimaging techniques including functional MRI, MR spectroscopy, and EEG.

Projects

Currently, I am investigating the effects of theta burst stimulation on various cortical regions and neural populations in vivo. In addition I use machine learning and simulations to quantify biomarkers of brain stimulation and evluate biophysical factors in order to optimise stimulation protocols.
