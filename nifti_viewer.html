Instructions

This viewer is pre-loaded with the MNI 152 standard brain.

How to load your data:

  1. Click "Load New Image" in the toolbar.
  2. Select your .nii.gz file.

Use 1-finger drag to adjust contrast/brightness. Use 2-finger scroll to navigate slices.
