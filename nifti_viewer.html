This viewer is pre-loaded with the MNI 152 standard brain. Scroll, hover or click on the brain to see different columes and anatomical labels.

You can also use the 'File' menu to 'Close All' and upload your own DICOM or NIfTI MRI image. If your MRI file is normalised to MNI space, the MNI template overlay will automatically add region labels to your image.
