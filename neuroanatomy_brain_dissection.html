Neuroanatomy (Ovine brain) Quiz

This quiz uses labelled still images from a dissected sheep (Ovis aries) brain and corresponding ex vivo MRI images from the same specimen. Because of the fixation/storage solution, MRI contrast may appear partly reversed compared with conventional in vivo MRI contrast. The specimen tissue was fixed in a solution composed of distilled water (94.73%), propylene glycol (3.84%), and phenoxyethanol (1.42%).

Dissection, research, photography, MR imaging and preprocessing, quiz preparation, and coding by Remy Cohan. © Remy Cohan; CohanR.GitHub.io.
